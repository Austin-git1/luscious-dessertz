Our Story

A labor of love.

Luscious Dessertz LLC is more than a dessert shop — it's a labor of love rooted in Adrian, Michigan. We believe that the finest moments in life deserve something extraordinary, and that's exactly what we pour into every single creation.

Our desserts are handcrafted fresh, made with quality ingredients, and presented with the kind of care you'd give to something truly precious. From our signature creations to rotating seasonal specials, every visit to Luscious Dessertz is an experience worth returning for.

We are a walk-in boutique — no menus online, no rush orders. Come as you are, discover what's fresh that day, and let us take care of the rest.

— Luscious Dessertz LLC
