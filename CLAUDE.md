## Design Direction

- **Aesthetic**: Cinematic, editorial, high-end dessert brand feel
- **Animations**: Hai Poke-style slide-in transitions on scroll/load
- **Location section**: Loro-style (full-bleed map or address block with atmosphere)
- **Mobile responsive**: First-class — many customers are on phones
- **No generic AI design patterns**: Every section should feel intentional and art-directed
- Warmth and luxury — cream and gold dominate the feel, black for drama, pink for personality

## Technical Requirements

- Pure HTML/CSS/JS (or lightweight framework if justified — no heavy CMS)
- Google Fonts: Cormorant Garamond
- Scroll-triggered animations via Intersection Observer or GSAP
- No online ordering system — prominently communicate walk-ins only
- Fast load, optimized images
- Semantic HTML for accessibility

## Goal

Build the best possible website for this business. Every design decision should serve the brand — cinematic, warm, editorial. Think luxury dessert boutique, not generic bakery template.
